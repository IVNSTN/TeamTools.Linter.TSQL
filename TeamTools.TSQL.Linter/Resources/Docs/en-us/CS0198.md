

# Circular action over table in trigger on the very same action

|||
|-|-|
| Id | **CS0198**
| Mnemo | TRIGGER_CIRCULAR_ACTION
| Severity | ⚠ Warning
| Category | [CodeSmell](./Category_CodeSmell.md)
| Source code | [TriggerCircularActionRule.cs](../../../Rules/CodeSmell/TriggerCircularActionRule.cs)

## Cause

<p id="descr">Ругается если, например, в триггере AFTER UPDATE происходит UPDATE той же таблицы. Или в AFTER INERT опять INSERT. Или в AFTER DELETE опять DELETE. Не надо так.</p>
