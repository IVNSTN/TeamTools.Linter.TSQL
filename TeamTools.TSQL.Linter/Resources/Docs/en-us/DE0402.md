

# Deprecated type is used

|||
|-|-|
| Id | **DE0402**
| Mnemo | DEPRECATED_TYPE
| Severity | ⚠ Warning
| Category | [Deprecation](./Category_Deprecation.md)
| Source code | [DeprecatedTypeRule.cs](../../../Rules/Deprecation/DeprecatedTypeRule.cs)

## Cause

<p id="descr">Replace deprecated data type.</p>

## Examples

Bad

```sql
DECLARE
	@Comment TEXT
	, @Description NTEXT
	, @DocImage IMAGE
	, @UpdateTime TIMESTAMP 
	, @Id dbo.TIndex 
	, @Value SQL_VARIANT
```

Good

```sql
DECLARE
	@Comment VARCHAR(MAX)
	, @Description NVARCHAR(MAX)
	, @DocImage VARBINARY
	, @Ver ROWVERSION 
	, @Id INT 
	do not use SQL_VARIANT
```

## Tips

💡 User-Defined data type may be deprecated.
